1. UserContoller
	- Login
		+ Receive username and password from HTTPrequest.
		+ Call AuthenticationService to check username and password.
		+ Call ProductServiceImpl to get all product in database.
		+ Send all product to BuyProduct controller.

2. ProductController
		- Check login session information 
		- Call ProductServiceImpl to get all product
		- Redirect to listproduct.jsp
		- Call ProductServiceImpl to get product by name when user "ADD to CART"
		- Set list product to session
		- Redirect to cartdetail.jsp
